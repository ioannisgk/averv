FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

It is critical for my infrastrure to be certified in terms of security. What are the steps that I need to follow?

First, choose the plan that is right for you and then click on the Pay Now button to start the application process. In order to complete this activity, you must pay the required amount. Once the payment is confirmed, our security experts will start the security assessment against your domain. If the results show that your application follows the latest security standards and your score is in the "A" zone, we will issue the Security Certification which will be valid for your domain and for a set period of time. Along with your certification, you will get a badge so that your application can be recognized globally as a certified secure application and it will be listed in the Certifications page.

I have just paid for the Standard Security package. How long will it take for the actual assessment to be completed?

When we receive your application and confirm the payment, you will be informed via email about the status of your application. This usually happens within 3 business days. If your application is approved, our security experts will start analyzing your infrastructure and then run the security tests. During the assessment you will never experience any downtime, since our sophisticated tools are designed to have as low impact as possible to the object under test. Within the next 5 business days, you will receive the official results in a form of a PDF file that includes all identified issues, their severity and recommendations. If your score is in the "A" zone, you will be Security Certified by AVERV.

I am having difficulties identifying the right Plan for me. What are the most important factors when deciding which Security Package to purchase?

The mmost important factors when deciding to buy a Security Package, are: (i) the validity period, which is for how long your certification will be valid, every Package has a validity period because on the new assessment the current security vulnerabilities in the world will change and we continuously update our databases to make sure that we follow the latest security standards, (ii) the number of applicable domains is also very important, because if your infrastructure consists of 2 or more endpoints, it is recommended to check them all of them to be sure that your whole application is assessed against the security threats, (iii) the renewal discount, which help you reduce your costs for future security assessments.

Your Security Packages from Plans & Pricing are too expensive for me. Do you offer any discounts?

We only offer one form of discount and this is the renewal discount. This is done due to the fact that our developers continuously develop custom tools for our security experts and they in turn need to trigger automated scans and manually check your infrastructure for the most common security vulnerabilities. This is a high cost for us, but we also try to be fair with you. This is why we offer renewal discounts, so you will not have to pay the full amount when you wish for a second security assessment after a few months. We believe that this is the right business model on which we can be trust with our clients.

Do you improve your product continuously? How can I be sure that my Security Certification has significant market value?

We are a dedicated team of people who continuously improve and update our product and services. In order to raise the quality even more, we have recently acquired the accredidation by Cloud Computing Security Association (CCSA). This means that our services are now recognized as standard compliant and you will get the best possible value for the money you invest in us. We update not only our inhouse tools, but also our processes and the details and recommendations on the final report that you will receive as a client. Our core base is updated twice a week with the most recent security vulnerabilities. This way you can be sure that when you receive a Security Certification from us, your infrastructure is fully secure.
